The Profile

A strategist built for bigger rooms.

Yvonne Spencer is a Memphis-based commercial real estate broker, development executive, and U.S. Army veteran — and the Founder of LeadHER™ CRE. Her practice spans heavy industrial land, landlord and tenant representation, commercial investments, infill redevelopment, strategic asset disposition, residential sales, business consulting, and credit specialization.

Across more than twenty years of executive leadership, Yvonne has operated where real estate meets public policy, capital, and community. That work includes leadership involvement in the 455-acre Fort McPherson redevelopment in Atlanta — a major public-private strategy converting a closed Army installation into a mixed-use economic engine.

"The Army taught me to read terrain before I move. Real estate is the same discipline — at the scale of a city."

Today she advises owners, investors, tenants, lenders, and economic-development stakeholders with institutional discipline and market intelligence — and through LeadHER™ CRE, she equips women, emerging agents, and community-minded professionals to make the same move she did: from transactions to transformation.
