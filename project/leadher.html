The Mission

More women in
bigger rooms.

Equipping women and emerging real estate professionals to move from residential transactions to commercial real estate leadership.

Commercial real estate runs on a different language — capital stacks, entitlement, underwriting, development strategy. LeadHER CRE teaches that language, surrounds members with mentorship, and builds the confidence to operate where the bigger decisions get made. Premium in standard, community in spirit.

Who LeadHER CRE
is built for.

Three audiences, one trajectory — toward development-minded leadership.

Audience 01

Women in real estate

Ready to claim a seat at the commercial and development table — with the models and mentorship to back it up.

Audience 02

Residential agents

Established in residential and ready to pivot into commercial brokerage, investments, and land.

Audience 03

Emerging professionals

Early-career and community-minded — building toward development-level leadership from the start.
